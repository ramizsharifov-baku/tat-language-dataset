Tat language materials in this repository were collected, structured, and
provided by Ramiz Sharif (Azerbaijan) in 2024–2026 as part of an initiative
for Tat language preservation, digitalization, and future use in Artificial
Intelligence and language technologies.

This repository represents an early structured digital collection of Tat
language materials prepared for open use in research, education, and
technology development.

Author: Ramiz Sharif
Country: Azerbaijan
Year: 2024–2026
