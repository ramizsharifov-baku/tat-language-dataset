One day I went to the mountain. 
The sun was shining and the wind was blowing. 
I saw a small house and an old man. 
The old man gave me bread and tea. 
We talked and I went back home in the evening.

Bir ruz men kuh-ra ravom.
Ru taftid va bad vazid.
Men kiçik xane va pir mard binom.
Pir mard ma nan va çay dod.
Ama gap zedom va men şam vaxti xane-ra bargashtom.
